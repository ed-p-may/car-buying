MA Registration
Must be done within 10 days of signing

⏱ MA law requires titling within 10 days of purchase. Don't delay. You cannot drive the car (no plates) until you have your MA registration.

MA is unique: your insurance agent must physically stamp your registration form before you can register. This step has to happen before the RMV visit.
